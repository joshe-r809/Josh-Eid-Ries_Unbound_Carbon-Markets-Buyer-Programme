02 / Philosophy
Three principles.
Everything flows from these.
01
Segment by maturity, not sector
Procurement stage determines what a buyer needs next. Sector is secondary.
02
Relationships before transactions
Nobody signs an offtake after a keynote. They sign after trust is built, peer-to-peer.
03
Events feed the year; the year feeds events
What happens between conferences determines what is possible when everyone is in the room.
OSCAR Contracts is the template: a workshop surfaces a shared pain, the right people connect, new market infrastructure is born. Carbon Unbound Buyer Workshop, 2025
03 / Buyer Architecture
Three tiers.
Three distinct programmes.
Explorer
New entrant
They need
  • Demystification, not pitches
  • Peer exposure to buyers one step ahead
  • A safe space to ask basic questions
Win: first meaningful supplier conversation initiated
Procurer
Active buyer
They need
  • Deal flow and vetted supplier access
  • MRV and contract confidence
  • Closed-door rooms to move fast
Win: purchase commitment or LOI within 6 months
Leader
Sophisticated buyer
They need
  • A platform, not a programme
  • Co-design and hosting opportunities
  • Influence on market direction
Win: repeat hosting, peer referrals into lower tiers
